Think of everything as system, receiving matter as input, processing it and outputting a new form.

The processing part is influenced by what the system let in and out of it. 

**What do you let yourself perceive?**

Allowing bad forms of information will lead to contamination of your system, thus, to decay. 

Be around a system that does not care about what they eat, you will be pulled towards them asking you to go eat at a fast food, ordering uber eats, skipping gym. 

Be around a system that spends their time watching tiktok, you will be pulled towards them showing you videos, talking about content creators, trends.

Be around a system that keeps procrastinating, doing the viable minimal, you will be pulled towards lazyness, contentment, mediocrity.

Those things are dots, strings attached to it and **associated** with the system you're letting sit around you.

**Scan your environment and cut the strings**
